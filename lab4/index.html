Baking Conchas

Get 3 ¾ cups all-purpose flour,  ½ cup + 2 tbs white sugar, 7.5 grams instant dry yeast, 
½ tsps salt, ½ cup butter, 100 ml eggs,1 tsps vanilla, ½ cup milk
½ cup + 1 tsps vegetable shortening, 100 grams confectioner sugar,5 grams cocoa powder 
Mix all dry ingredients ( 3 ¾ cups all purpose flour, ½ cup + 2 tbs white sugar, ½ tsps salt,) 
in bowl and mix well
Add butter and mixAdd your eggs, and vanilla, and mix until you reach a dough consistency
Keep mixing for a while
After 10ish minutes, you will have a soft texture (somewhat sticky)
Kneed your dough and shape into a ball-like shape
Get a bowl covered in grease, put dough in, and let rest for 2 hours with plastic wrap
Preheat oven to 325
Let dough rise in a warm place like the top of a stove
Make toppings -> Mix vegetable shorting in a bowl until creamy
Add confectioner sugar and a bit of flour so it doesn't get too sticky
Add your flavor powder (coco powder)
Mix ingredients well and set aside
Once it is ready, divide the dough into smaller balls 
Put the dough into a greased pan with space in between each ball.
Flatten out the topping and make strips to put on top of top of the dough
Have fun with it, maybe cut the strips
Let rise a bit more then put in the oven for 2 hours
Then put them in the oven for 15-20 minutes
Then get a gold consistency on the top, edges, or bottom
Then when they turn golden, take them out of the oven, and DONE
